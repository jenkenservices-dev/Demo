About SMS BASIC Scores

Last Updated: September 2025

The Safety Measurement System (SMS) uses inspection data to monitor carrier safety performance across five BASIC (Behavior Analysis and Safety Improvement Category) categories. Percentile rankings from 0-100 indicate relative performance, with lower scores indicating better safety performance compared to similar carriers.
